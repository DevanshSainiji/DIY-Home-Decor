The latest trend in glasses is gold rimmed drinking glasses. They were very popular in the 90s, and the trend is back. Since I decided to downsize my budget for my desires, I'm going to make easy DIY gold rimmed cocktail glasses.

So, how to paint gold rimmed glasses? The DIY gold rimmed glasses can be obtained by following these 8 simple steps.

Materials

Glass

Golden glass paint

Plate

Duct tape

Step 1 - Stick adhesive tape to the glass

We don't plan on painting the entire glass gold, so wrap some tape around the glass, leaving only a small part of the rim free. This will give you a symmetrical outline.

Step 2 - Wrapped Glass

Once you have wrapped the glass all around with tape, your glass will look like this.

Step 3 - Pour the paint onto a plate

We will not use the traditional painting technique with a brush. Instead, we will dip the upside-down glass into the paint.
There are two reasons why we use this technique
1) It saves a lot of time.
2) It creates an even layer of colour over the glass' entire edge.
The latter is the main reason for dipping into the paint.

Step 4 - Place the rim of the glass on the plate

Now, the rim of the glass is dipped into the plate filled with golden glass paint.

Step 5 - Let the paint drip off

Remove the jar from the paint and leave it upside down for a few minutes to allow the paint to drip off and dry thoroughly.

Step 6 - Wait for the paint to dry

Once the excess paint has dripped off, let the glass dry for a few hours.

Step 7 - Remove the tape

Carefully remove the tape from the glass when the paint is completely dry. Be careful not to let the tape take off the paint.

Step 8 - The glass with the gold rim is now ready for use
